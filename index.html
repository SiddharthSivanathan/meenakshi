Our Story

A Local Favorite for
Authentic Vegetarian Food

Meenaakshi Cafe Corner is a beloved vegetarian cafe located at the Kottampatti corner. Loved by locals and travelers alike, the cafe is celebrated for its delicious South Indian snacks, affordable meals, and genuine warm hospitality.

Though small in size, the cafe is big in flavor. Every dish is freshly prepared and served hot, making it the perfect stop for tea, snacks, or a wholesome quick meal during your journey.
